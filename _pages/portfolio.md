## TidyTuesday

TidyTuesday is a weekly data challenge aimed at the R community. Every week, a new dataset is posted alongside a chart or article related to that dataset, encouraging participants to explore the data.

Below is a selection of my contributions to the #TidyTuesday challenge. All contributions and the code used to generate them can be found on GitHub.
